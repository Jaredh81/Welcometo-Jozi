Security & Alarm System

Weve set up a simple alarm system to give you peace of mind during your stay. How to Use the Alarm As you enter the property, look up on the right side of the patio roof, you'll see a small, bright blue light. This lets you know the alarm status. If the blue light is ON, the alarm is armed and outdoor security beams are active. If the blue light is OFF, the alarm is disarmed. To Disarm the Alarm Press the blue button on the remote (labelled Arm/Disarm Alarm). The blue light will switch OFF youre good to go in and out. To Arm the Alarm Again Once youre inside and settled, press the blue button again. The blue light will turn back ON, and the alarm will re-arm the outdoor beams. Note: The alarm wont be triggered by movement on the patio so you can relax outside even when its armed.

Nature & Outdoors

Melville Koppies Nature Reserve

Melville Koppies Nature Reserve Escape the city without leaving it! The Melville Koppies are a protected heritage site and nature reserve right here in the heart of Johannesburg. Think winding trails, sweeping city views, indigenous flora, and a fascinating glimpse into our areas ancient geology and history. Sunday Guided Hikes Every Sunday morning, the Koppies hosts a guided hike led by knowledgeable volunteers. Youll learn about the plants, wildlife, and history while getting in some fresh air and exercise. Hikes usually start around 08:30, last 23 hours, and cover a range of terrains so wear comfy walking shoes, bring water, and dont forget a hat. Need-to-know: Entrance for guided hikes is at Judith Road, Emmarentia. Theres a small entrance fee (cash only) that goes toward maintaining the reserve. No dogs, picnics, or cycling the Koppies are kept wild and natural.

Botanical Gardens (Emmarentia)

Just a few minutes from Melville, this vast green space is where Joburg locals come to breathe. The park is divided into two main areas: Emmarentia Dam A tranquil spot for walking, jogging, or simply sitting by the water. Youll often see canoeists gliding across the dam, families feeding ducks, and locals enjoying picnics under the shade of giant trees. Johannesburg Botanical Gardens Home to themed gardens including a fragrant Rose Garden with over 4,500 rose bushes, a Herb Garden, and an Indigenous Garden showcasing South African flora. Why Go: Perfect for a relaxed morning stroll, a lazy afternoon picnic, or an active jog. On weekends, youll often find yoga classes, dog walkers, and live music pop-ups. Good to Know: Open daily, sunrise to sunset Free entry Parking available at several gates (the Olifants Road entrance is closest to Melville) Pack a blanket, snacks, and sunscreen for the perfect Joburg park day

Load Shedding & Solar Power

In South Africa, we sometimes experience load shedding planned power outages to help manage the electricity grid. Dont worry, our cottage is solar-powered with battery backup, so the essentials will keep running when the grid goes down. However, the solar battery only lasts for a certain amount of time. To make sure it gets you through the outage, please: Use only the essentials (lights, Wi-Fi, phone charging). Avoid high-power appliances during load shedding (e.g., oven, kettle, hairdryer, heaters). Keep lights you arent using switched off. Well let you know the load shedding schedule during your stay so you can plan around it. Tip: Load shedding usually lasts 24 hours at a time, and the solar kicks in automatically, so theres no need to do anything just help us save power so the backup lasts until the electricity returns.

Soweto Bicycle Tours Pedal through Sowetos streets, history & culture. Quick to move, easy to connect, unforgettable to experience.


Walter Sisulu Botanical Gardens Escape the city buzz at one of Joburgs most loved green spaces. a waterfall, and sprawling gardens, its the perfect spot for a picnic, birdwatching, or a gentle walk. 23km / Entrance fee  View Map


Victoria Yards An urban oasis of art studios, veggie gardens, street art & community spirit. Grab a coffee and wander. victoriayards.co.za


PopArt Theatre Joburgs stage for raw, fearless creativity. Bold plays & experiments in an intimate new Parkwood space. popartcentre.co.za


Roving Bantu Kitchen More than an eatery: its a cultural time capsule with food, stories & music that scream Joburg soul. rovingbantu.co.za


Troyeville Hotel Legendary Portuguese chicken & seafood with one of the best skyline views in the city. troyevillehotel.co.za


Fordsburg Market Dive into Joburgs Indian subculture with spice stalls, sweet shops, and street food. Best on weekends. - 



The Radium Beerhall
A 1920s beerhall thats survived through decades. Live music, century-old architecture, and a real piece of Joburg history.
